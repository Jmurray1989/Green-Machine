About-Us

Green-Machine is founded on a simple idea that all communities deserve access to smart, affordable mobility.
Our aim is to reduce dependence on personal automobiles for short distance transportation and leave future generations with a cleaner, healthier planet. Our scooters allow you to enjoy a safe, fun and easy journey to wherever you want to go while reducing your carbon footprint! All our models are lightweight, noise free, easy to ride and foldable, providing you with a cost effective and smooth method of transport!
We are a 100% Irish owned company.
